Bangtan Sonyeondan


World Wide famous Boy Band KPOP Group


BTS stands for Bangtan Sonyeondan.

It is also known as the Bangtan Boys.

It is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed in 2013 in seoul in Bighit Entertainment. Bangtan Sonyeondan in Korean means "bulletproof boy scouts". Koreans call them by this name, however people in other countires refer to them as BTS


RM

Kim Namjoon born september 12,1994. He is the leader of the group(rapper). He is better known by his stage name RM (Rap Monster).
